Complaints Procedure

Last updated: 1 January 2025

Double Paradigm Ltd is committed to providing the highest standard of service to all our clients and partners. We take complaints seriously and aim to resolve all concerns promptly, fairly, and professionally. If you are dissatisfied with any aspect of our service, please follow the procedure outlined below.

Our commitment to you:

  • Every complaint will be acknowledged within 2 working days
  • All complaints will be investigated thoroughly and impartially
  • We will keep you informed of progress throughout the process
  • We will provide a final response within 28 working days
  • We will use your feedback to improve our services

Step 1: Informal Resolution (Days 1–5)

In the first instance, please contact us directly. Many concerns can be resolved quickly through a simple conversation. We encourage you to reach out to us as soon as possible so we can address your concerns promptly.

Contact details for informal resolution:

  • Email: hello@smiledentalcare.co.uk
  • Phone: 020 7946 0123 (Mon–Fri 8am–7pm, Sat 9am–5pm)
  • Post: Double Paradigm Ltd, Harley Street, London, UK

When contacting us, please provide:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • A clear description of your complaint
  • The date(s) the issue occurred
  • Any relevant documentation or correspondence
  • What outcome you are seeking

We aim to resolve all informal complaints within 5 working days of receipt.

Step 2: Formal Written Complaint (Days 6–28)

If your concern is not resolved informally, or if you prefer to make a formal complaint from the outset, please submit your complaint in writing. A formal complaint can be made by:

  • Email: hello@smiledentalcare.co.uk with the subject line "Formal Complaint"
  • Post: Marked "Private and Confidential — Formal Complaint", Double Paradigm Ltd, Harley Street, London, UK

Acknowledgement

We will acknowledge receipt of your formal complaint within 2 working days. The acknowledgement will include:

  • Confirmation that your complaint has been received
  • The name of the person handling your complaint
  • An expected timeline for our response
  • A reference number for your complaint

Investigation

A senior member of our team will investigate your complaint thoroughly. This may involve:

  • Reviewing all relevant documentation and correspondence
  • Speaking with team members involved in your case
  • Consulting with external advisors if necessary
  • Contacting you for additional information if required

Final Response

We will provide a written final response within 28 working days of receiving your formal complaint. Our response will:

  • Confirm our findings from the investigation
  • Explain the outcome of your complaint
  • Detail any action we will take to resolve the matter
  • Outline any changes we will make to prevent similar issues

If we are unable to provide a final response within 28 working days, we will write to you explaining the reason for the delay and provide an updated timeline.

Step 3: Escalation to the GDC

If your complaint relates to a dental professional's fitness to practise or clinical standards, and you are dissatisfied with our response, you have the right to escalate your complaint to the General Dental Council (GDC).

The GDC is the UK regulator for dental professionals. They can investigate concerns about the fitness to practise of dentists, dental nurses, dental technicians, dental hygienists, dental therapists, orthodontic therapists, and clinical dental technicians.

General Dental Council contact details:

  • Website: www.gdc-uk.org
  • Email: information@gdc-uk.org
  • Phone: 020 7167 6000
  • Address: 37 Wimpole Street, London, W1G 8DQ

Our GDC Registered Partner Number: [GDC000000]

Alternative Dispute Resolution

If your complaint concerns a commercial dispute, you may also wish to consider Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR). The following bodies may be able to assist:

  • CEDR (Centre for Effective Dispute Resolution): www.cedr.com
  • The Ombudsman Association: www.ombudsmanassociation.org

Data Protection Complaints

If your complaint relates specifically to how we have handled your personal data, you may contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint
  • Phone: 0303 123 1113
  • Address: Information Commissioner's Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire, SK9 5AF

Feedback and Continuous Improvement

We regard every complaint as an opportunity to improve. All complaints are reviewed by our senior team and used to enhance our processes, training, and service delivery. We are grateful for feedback, as it helps us maintain and improve the quality of service we provide to all our clients.

Monitoring and Review

This complaints procedure is reviewed annually and updated as necessary. The current version was last reviewed on 1 January 2025. If you have any suggestions for improving this procedure, please contact us.
