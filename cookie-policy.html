Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Double Paradigm Ltd ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website at doubleparadigm.co.uk. We use CookieYes to manage your cookie preferences and ensure compliance with UK GDPR and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies cannot harm your device or access files stored on it.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting privacy preferences or filling in forms. These do not require your consent.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
cookieyes-consent CookieYes Records user cookie consent preferences 1 year
__cf_bm Cloudflare Bot protection and security 30 minutes

2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our website. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies, we will not know when you have visited our site and will not be able to monitor its performance.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Persists session state 2 years

2.3 Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set through our website by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other websites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
_fbp Meta (Facebook) Tracks visits across websites for advertising 3 months
li_fat_id LinkedIn LinkedIn advertising and conversion tracking 30 days

3. How to Manage Your Cookie Preferences

3.1 Via Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a CookieYes cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or manage your preferences in detail. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.

3.2 Via Your Browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete specific or all cookies
  • Block all cookies or cookies from specific websites
  • Allow cookies from specific websites (whitelisting)

Please note that blocking all cookies may prevent some features of our website from working correctly. Browser-specific instructions can be found at:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

3.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services. We do not have control over these cookies and encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties:

  • CookieYes Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy
